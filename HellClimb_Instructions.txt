Hell Climb – CSC 329 – Gameplay Instructions
‘D’ – Move Right
‘A’ – Move Left
‘Space’ OR ‘Right-Mouse Click’ – Jump
‘M’ – Mute Music
Additional Information:
- Player may go off camera horizontally and appear on the other side
- If an obstacle blocks a player from switching sides, they will die and restart
- Cannonballs can also be used to help you climb faster
